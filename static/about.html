About Me

Hello! I'm Anjana Shaju who's passionate about learning and sharing everything I know about DevOps and Cloud technologies.

I am sharing this youtube links to help beginners like me get started with tools like Docker, Kubernetes, Jenkins, GitHub Actions, and cloud platforms like AWS and Azure — without needing a paid course.

Everything I share here is based on real learning experiences, hands-on labs, and free resources I’ve personally used and found helpful.

My goal is to build a community of learners who support each other while mastering DevOps together. Whether you’re just starting out or looking to improve your skills, I hope this site gives you a helpful push in the right direction.

Thanks for visiting, and happy learning!
